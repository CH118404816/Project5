Sport

A description of my long and fruitful career of various sports.

Hurling Rugby Boxing

Hurling

I began my Hurling and GAA life playing with Blareny GAA, after two years I left Blarney in search of greener pastures. I started my GAA career in bishopstown in 2006 and remained there until 2017 where I decided to quit with State Examinations Looming. For the best part of my time in Bishopstown, I played Corner Back, where I proved myself to be a reliable and nippy asset

Rugby

I began my days of rugby playing with HighField Rugby and Football Club. I stayed there for a year until 2012 where I entered PBC. I floated the B and C teams, rolling through my years in Pres. I found that I wasnt really good enough to be A content but I was also not poor enough for the D's

I played rugby right up and until Barry cup where I only attended the training sessions for the entire year. That also ended in 2017 and then I finished playing field sport.

Boxing

I took up Boxing when I entered University. It was both a fun and painful experience as I wasn't very good initially. Although, after attending a series of important sessions, I started to win a number of fights and began to prove myself, particularly if I was an under dog.
